Natural Teamplayer

Since it is so much more fun!

Back when I was at high school, I led a small hobbyist game developer team. These were not only my first step in game programming, but also in organization and leading.

As I started studying in Magdeburg, I joined Acagamics, a small student game developer club. We founded a registered non-profit organization and I became its first elected chairman. Today, Acagamics has more than one hundred members. The club organizes excursions, guest lectures, GameJams and more. Working together with our university we give lectures for students.

As one of the most experienced senior members, I still attend the monthly organizational meetings to help and consult.

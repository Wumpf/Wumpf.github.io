Passionate Graphics Programmer

Shiny graphics and fun for everyone!

Since I taught myself programming in 2005, I have made many games and graphic demos in my free time.

Empowering Teamplay

Working together is so much more fun!

Back when I was at high school, I led a hobbyist game developer team. These were not only my first step in game programming, but also in organization and teamwork.

As I started studying in Magdeburg, I joined Acagamics, a small student game developer club. We founded a registered non-profit organization and I became its first elected chairman. Today, Acagamics has more than one hundred members. The club organizes courses, excursions, guest lectures, GameJams and more.

Like Acagamics, most of my achievements are tied to powerful teams that made them possible in the first place. Few things are more rewarding than working together on a shared vision.

Education & Science

Always learning something new!

Last year I finished my bachelor degree as best graduate in my field at the Otto-von-Guericke University. I wrote my thesis at Havok about realtime particle lighting.

Currently I am writing my master thesis about a new realtime global illumination technique.

Meanwhile I am finishing a scientific paper for the EGSR about speeding up Photon Mapping without quality loss through clever mesh reduction.
